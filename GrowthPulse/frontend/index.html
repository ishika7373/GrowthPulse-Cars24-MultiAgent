How GrowthPulse decides what to fix and why.

Each query enters a Router classifier, dispatches to one of four scoped specialists (Campaign · Audience · Bidding · Budget), and a Supervisor synthesises cross-domain questions into a single, prioritised action plan — citing every claim back to the specialist that surfaced it.
